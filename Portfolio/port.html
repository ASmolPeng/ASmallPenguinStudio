Title Sequence

Details

The assignment was to create a minimum 0:30 animation in After Effects and this animation was to be a title sequence for a show or movie of their choosing. Students could not use music or images that are used in the original media and instead had to find/create music and imagery by themselves. I chose the anime Shōjo Kageki: Revue Starlight. I created a character-focused title sequence that was light-hearted and cute which is similar to the original title sequence but doesn't go into the more dramatic moments of the anime.

Software

  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe After Effects

Shape Dance Party

Details

The assignment was to create a 0:30 amination in After Effects using stock music and simple geometric shapes. I used a song called Coffee Morning by Olga Scotland. The image that I thought of while listing to this song was a stroll though a city in the early morning, so I used building shapes that would pop up to the beat of the song, a color palette similar to a sunrise, and a animated line that represented the flow of the melody of the song.
